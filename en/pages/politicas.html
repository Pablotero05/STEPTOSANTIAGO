Section 7

Your Data Protection Rights

Under the GDPR and Spanish LOPDGDD, you are entitled to exercise the following rights:

A

Access (Art. 15)

Know what personal data we hold about you and obtain a copy.

R

Rectification (Art. 16)

Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

E

Erasure — "Right to be forgotten" (Art. 17)

Request deletion of your data when it is no longer necessary.

L

Restriction (Art. 18)

Temporarily suspend processing under certain circumstances.

P

Portability (Art. 20)

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format and transfer it to another controller.

O

Objection (Art. 21)

Object to processing, especially for direct marketing purposes.

Section 8

Protection of Minors' Data

Our services are not directed at children under 14 years of age. Under Article 7 of the Spanish LOPDGDD, individuals aged 14 and over may provide their own consent. For children under 14, consent from a parent or legal guardian is required.

If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 14 without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it immediately. If you are aware of such a case, please notify us at privacy@steptosantiago.com.
